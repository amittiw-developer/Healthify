Privacy Policy

Healthify may collect information you provide, such as name, email, age, height, weight, profile selections, lifestyle inputs, symptoms, and health history entries. This information is used to provide wellness awareness features inside the app.

How Data Is Used

  • To generate health score and awareness reports.
  • To personalize profile and wellness guidance.
  • To maintain authentication and app access.

Safety and Sensitive Data

Do not enter highly sensitive information unless necessary. Healthify does not provide medical diagnosis and should not be used for emergencies. Authentication may be handled through third-party providers such as Supabase or Google depending on configuration.

User Responsibility

Users are responsible for keeping passwords, OTPs, and account access secure. Healthify cannot guarantee that AI-generated guidance will be complete, accurate, or suitable for every individual medical situation.

For medical concerns, consult a qualified healthcare professional. For emergencies, contact local emergency services immediately.
