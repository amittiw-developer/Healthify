How it works

A simple, honest system for habit improvement.

  • Create only one active copy of a habit.
  • Day 1 unlocks after 2 hours so you get time to actually work on it.
  • Each day gives a different reflection prompt. Write what you did, then Healthify marks the day complete.
  • Future days stay locked and missed days remain visible.
