By using Healthify, you agree that the app is intended for general health awareness, wellness education, and personal tracking support only.

No Medical Diagnosis

Healthify does not diagnose, treat, cure, prevent disease, prescribe medicines, or replace doctors, hospitals, emergency services, or qualified healthcare professionals.

User Responsibility

  • Use the app responsibly and provide accurate information.
  • Do not ignore severe or worsening symptoms based on app output.
  • Seek professional medical advice before making health decisions.
  • For emergencies, contact emergency services immediately.

AI and Report Limitations

AI responses and generated reports may be incomplete or inaccurate and are based only on the information entered by the user. They should be treated as awareness guidance, not clinical advice.

Emergency Notice: If you experience chest pain, breathing difficulty, fainting, stroke-like symptoms, severe bleeding, self-harm thoughts, or any urgent condition, seek immediate medical help.
