About Healthify

Healthify is a wellness awareness platform designed to help users understand basic health signals, lifestyle patterns, and general self-care guidance. It offers profile tracking, health score tools, AI wellness conversation, and report-style summaries for awareness.

Important: Healthify is not a medical diagnosis app, not a doctor, not an emergency service, and not a replacement for qualified healthcare professionals. It is intended only for knowledge, awareness, and responsible wellness guidance.

If symptoms are severe, persistent, worsening, or related to chest pain, breathing difficulty, fainting, severe bleeding, stroke-like symptoms, self-harm, pregnancy concerns, or any emergency, users should seek immediate professional medical care.
